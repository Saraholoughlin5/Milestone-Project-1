Freeway Farmer's Market

About the Market

History

The Freeway Farmer's Market was founded in March 1998 by Pat Mullins and her husband Christopher Mullins. The Mullins family started out with one stall in the Ennis Town market, hoping to sell excess vegetables from their modest farm at home in Scarriff.

Enquiries from other farmers and local suppliers prompted Pat to rent out two more stalls, selling cheese and fruit. Over the years the increase in population and changes in the layout of the town meant that relocating to a more accessible location was a must. The Freeway Farmer's Market started trading at Roslevan in Jume 2010, adding a wide variety of products to their growing number of stalls, and providing additional amenities for sellers and customers alike, including ample parking and refreshment areas.

Today, the market boasts 15 stalls, which provide honey, cheeses, cured meats, pickled vegetables, fruit, jams and more. The wares are produced locally, flavoured by the variety of the towns many cultures.

We hope you enjoy visiting us and supporting your local vendors!

Products

The Freeway Farmer's Market provides customers with a wide variety of homegrown and homemade products. The majority of the fruits, vegetables and herbs for sale at our stalls are organic, and are labelled as such. All stallholder's wares are produced by them, and county boundaries for produce sourcing is enforced.

Many of our stallholders grow fruit and vegetables year round at their own private gardens or small farms, or allotment areas. Handmade items, such as aprons, wreathes, flower-arrangments, wax paper and more, are created using locally sourced materials, such as beeswax, wool, parchment, twine, wood, plants, and more.

The variety and quality of produce for sale at our market is a source of great pride. We are grateful to have the opportunity to facilitate the sellers in getting their beautiful produce into the hands of locals and tourists alike.

We thank our vendors for consistently providing such amazing food, gifts and homeware to our customers, and we thank you for shopping local!
